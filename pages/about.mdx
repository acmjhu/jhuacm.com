# About us

We are a student organization of the [Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/) dedicated to furthering the knowledge and advancement of computers and information technology through the free exchange of ideas and information.

As a chapter of the oldest computing society in the world, the JHU ACM is a place for diverse backgrounds and interests, and serves the JHU community as a whole.

During the semester, the ACM has weekly meetings in Malone announced via email and posted on our [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/JHUACM/) page and the [Events](/events) section of this website.
